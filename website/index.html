Built for the thru-hike, not the day hike

Three trails. One file. Everything that matters at mile 1,276.

📅

A real itinerary engine

Pace, zero days, town breaks, end-date math — plan Springer to Katahdin or any segment between any two waypoints, then download the PDF. Free.

📈

On-Trail View

Full elevation profiles with every water source, shelter, and resupply pinned to its mile — plus a live GPS dot that knows your trail mile. Expand to full screen: profile and map, side by side.

🗺

Honest maps

Your segment on a real map with a real trail line. Lost the blaze? One tap opens maximum-zoom satellite on your exact position. And when you have zero bars, the data that matters never needed the internet anyway.

💧

Water intel from hikers ahead

Condition reports color the sources — flowing, fair, dry — on the profile and the map. Know before you carry.

🥾

The Hiker Board

The only real partner-finder in thru-hiking: direction, pace, dates, gear style. Find your trail family before the approach trail.

✨

Trail Magic

Trail Angels list what they offer at which road crossing; hikers find magic nearest-first. Nobody else has this. We checked.

⭐

All Treks — every state

76 named treks across all 50 states, honestly tiered: real geometry where we have it, a blank tape where we don't — and your GPS recording can write a trek's first profile. Don't see your trail? Add it.

🤫

Privacy as architecture

Plans live on your device. Your GPS position never leaves your phone. No analytics, no ad pixels, no data sales. We couldn't track you if we wanted to — so we built it so we can't want to.
