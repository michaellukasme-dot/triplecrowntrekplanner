Looking for a Leapfrog Partner
→
Leapfrog: 2 hikers, 2 cars. Park one car forward at the end, ride together to the start, hike to the forward car, then shuttle back for the second. Repeat by leg, paced to your FROM/TO and predicted arrival — so you each hike light, no full pack.
